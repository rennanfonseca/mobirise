About Me

Born in Brazil, Rennan Fonseca started his career in photography and social media in 2017. He began creating and publishing his photos on Instagram which shortly have been shared by hundreds of pages and magazines worldwide. He works with brands all over the world.


With fashion, product, portrait and travel approach, Rennan Fonseca offers in his pictures and travel stories creative, colorful, vibrant and positive vibes of the countries he visits, often unknown by the public.
